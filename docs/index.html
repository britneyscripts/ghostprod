01 · the problem and the strategy

Business data intelligence requires more than just attracting traffic; it requires ensuring positioning in the new AI ecosystems. Today, agents like ChatGPT and Gemini decide which products to compare based purely on structured data density and your page's technical performance. Poor technical performance, missing Schema.org, and descriptions without verifiable attributes make a PDP semantically invisible. The product exists on your server, but for the language model recommending the purchase, it simply doesn't exist.

03 · data engineering: under the hood

Invisibility is not solved with guesswork, but with data orchestration. The Ghostprod engine executes a deterministic pipeline that crosses real field data (CrUX) with real-time synthetic tests, validation of JSON-LD and natural language processing (NLP). It is engineering translating the chaos of code into intelligence actionable.
